Data Science & Physics · Northeastern University · Boston, MA

I am an undergraduate at Northeastern University studying Data Science and Physics, originally from Los Altos, California. Playing Division I soccer initially brought me to Boston, and over the past few years I've had the opportunity to explore and develop many of my interests.

My work focuses on how data-driven techniques and machine learning can help us better understand the physical world. I am an undergraduate researcher studying motor and cognitive function, and I'm particularly interested in fusion as a future energy source.

Outside of research, I enjoy tennis, golf, cooking, and traveling.
